CRAFTING
Permanence

With over a decade of dedication to the needle and ink, OBSIDIAN is not just a studio—it is an exploration of the human canvas. Founded on the principles of geometric precision and neo-traditional darkness, our work seeks to bridge the gap between ancient ritual and modern aesthetic.

Every piece is a collaborative journey, a visceral translation of personal narrative into permanent art. We believe in the weight of the line and the depth of the shadow.
